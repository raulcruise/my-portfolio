Computer Building

Recently I have been building many of my friends' gaming computers for them. To me, it's almost like putting together a huge puzzle except I know where each piece has to go. Yet that doesn't take away any satisfaction from seeing the end result. I always know roughly how the computer will look but I always love seeing the result that has come out of my work.
